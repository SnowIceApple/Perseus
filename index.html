UNIQUE CHALLENGES FOR CONNECTED CARS

  • Growing Complexity

    Connected cars are equipped with up to 120 SoCs,
    and autonomous vehicles are expected to be even
    more complex.

  • Security issues

    Currently, security solutions pose an increasing
    hacking threat to vehicle connected services and
    data transmission protection.

  • Linux Booting speed

    Boot speed can be up to 15 seconds, which can
    degrade performance and cause serious safety
    problems.
